CISCE ICSE 10th & ISC 12th Result 2025

Important Information

The Council for the Indian School Certificate Examination (CISCE) has officially released the ICSE Class 10th and ISC Class 12th examination results for 2025. Over 2.5 lakh students across India can now check their subject-wise scores online. The board examinations were conducted between February and April 2025, with results being declared within 45 days of exam completion.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. When was the CISCE ICSE/ISC Result 2025 declared?

The CISCE board declared the ICSE Class 10 and ISC Class 12 results on 30 April 2025 at 11:00 AM.

2. How can I check my CISCE result if I forgot my Unique ID?

Contact your school administration for your Unique ID. Schools have access to all student IDs and can provide this information.

3. What is the passing percentage for CISCE board exams?

Students need to score a minimum of 35% marks in each subject and an overall aggregate of 35% to pass the CISCE examinations.

4. How can I apply for revaluation of my CISCE marksheet?

You can apply for revaluation through your school from 1-10 May 2025. The application form with fee payment needs to be submitted to the CISCE board.

5. When will the original marksheet be available?

Original marksheets are usually distributed by schools within 2-3 weeks after the online result declaration. Contact your school for exact dates.

6. What should I do if there's an error in my result?

Immediately inform your school principal who will contact the CISCE board for corrections. Keep a printout of the online result for reference.

7. How can I improve my grades if I failed in one subject?

CISCE offers compartment exams in June 2025 for students who failed in 1-2 subjects. You can appear for these exams to improve your score.

8. Is the online marksheet valid for college admissions?

Most colleges accept the online printout for provisional admission, but you'll need to submit the original marksheet when available for final admission.

9. How to calculate percentage from CISCE grades?

CISCE provides marks out of 100 for each subject. Add marks of all subjects (excluding additional subjects) and divide by total number of subjects.

10. What documents do I need to collect my original marksheet?

You'll need to show your school ID card or admit card to collect the original marksheet from your school. Some schools may require a signed acknowledgment.
