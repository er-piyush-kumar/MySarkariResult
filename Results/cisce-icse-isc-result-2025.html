CISCE ICSE 10th & ISC 12th Result 2025

Published on: 30 April 2025 | 11:00 AM

Important Information

The Council for the Indian School Certificate Examination (CISCE) has officially released the ICSE Class 10th and ISC Class 12th examination results for 2025. Over 2.5 lakh students across India can now check their subject-wise scores online. The board examinations were conducted between February and April 2025, with results being declared within 45 days of exam completion.

Important Dates

  • ISC XII Exam: 13 Feb - 5 Apr 2025
  • ICSE X Exam: 18 Feb - 27 Mar 2025
  • Result Date: 30 Apr 2025 (11 AM)

Key Information

  • Conducting Body: CISCE Board
  • Result Status: Declared
  • Marks Available: Online & SMS

For ICSE Class 10th Students

How to Check Result

  1. Visit CISCE official website
  2. Click on "ICSE Result 2025" link
  3. Enter your Unique ID
  4. Submit to view marksheet
  5. Download/Print for future reference

For ISC Class 12th Students

How to Check Result

  1. Go to results.cisce.org
  2. Select "ISC Result 2025"
  3. Input your Candidate ID
  4. Enter CAPTCHA code
