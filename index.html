              Highest Paying Government Jobs in 2025

              The Indian government offers numerous high-salary positions across various sectors. Here are some of the most lucrative career options:

              1. Indian Administrative Services (IAS) & Indian Foreign Services (IFS)

              IAS officers start with a monthly salary of ₹56,100 (Level 10) which can grow to ₹2,50,000 for Cabinet Secretary (Level 18). IFS officers enjoy similar pay scales along with foreign postings benefits including housing, education allowances, and diplomatic privileges.

              2. Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs)

              Companies like ONGC, IOCL, BHEL, and NTPC offer starting packages of ₹12-15 lakhs per annum for entry-level engineers through GATE scores.

              3. Reserve Bank of India (RBI) Grade B Officers

              Starting salary of ₹77,208 per month with rapid growth potential to ₹1,30,000 within a few years, along with excellent banking sector benefits.

              4. Defense Services (NDA, CDS)

              Lieutenants in Army/Navy/Air Force start with ₹56,100-1,77,500 per month plus field area allowances, ration, and other military benefits.
