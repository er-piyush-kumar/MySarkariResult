Selection Process

The selection of candidates will be made through the following stages:

Online Examination

The online test will consist of:

  • Professional Knowledge (100 marks)
  • English Language (50 marks)
  • General Awareness (50 marks)
  • Total Duration: 2 hours

Interview

Candidates shortlisted in the online exam will be called for interview carrying 100 marks.

  • Minimum qualifying marks: 40% (35% for SC/ST/OBC/PWD)
  • Final merit list based on online test + interview marks

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the probation period for selected candidates?

Selected candidates will be on probation for 2 years from the date of joining. During probation period, performance will be closely monitored and confirmation is subject to satisfactory performance.

2. Is there any bond period for this recruitment?

Yes, selected candidates will be required to execute a bond to serve the Bank for a minimum period of 3 years from the date of joining, failing which they will be liable to pay liquidated damages of ₹2,00,000/- plus GST.

3. Can final year students apply for these posts?

No, candidates must have completed their qualifying degree as on 01/04/2025. Final year/semester students whose results are awaited are not eligible to apply.

4. What is the expected joining date for selected candidates?

The tentative joining date is expected to be around September-October 2025, subject to completion of all selection formalities. Exact date will be communicated to selected candidates.

5. Is there any negative marking in the online examination?

Yes, there will be negative marking of 0.25 marks for each wrong answer in the online examination. Unanswered questions will not attract any negative marking.

6. What documents are required during the application process?

You will need scanned copies of:

  • Photograph and Signature
  • Educational Certificates (Mark sheets & Degree)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • PwD Certificate (if applicable)
  • Experience Certificates (if any)

7. Can I apply for both Credit and IT posts?

No, candidates can apply for only one post - either Assistant Manager (Credit) or Assistant Manager (IT). Multiple applications will lead to rejection of all applications.

8. What is the syllabus for the online examination?

The syllabus includes:

  • Professional Knowledge: Banking, Finance, Accounting, IT concepts (as per post applied)
  • English Language: Grammar, Vocabulary, Comprehension
  • General Awareness: Banking/Financial Awareness, Current Affairs
Detailed syllabus is available in the official notification.

9. Will there be any training after joining?

Yes, selected candidates will undergo an intensive training program at Union Bank's training centers. The duration of training will be approximately 4-6 weeks, which may include classroom sessions and on-the-job training.

10. What are the career prospects after joining as Assistant Manager?

Union Bank offers excellent career growth opportunities. Assistant Managers can progress to higher grades through departmental promotions or by clearing internal examinations. The bank also provides opportunities for specialization and overseas postings based on performance.
