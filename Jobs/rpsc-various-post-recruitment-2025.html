About RPSC and Recruitment 2025

What is RPSC?

The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) is the premier recruiting agency for the state government of Rajasthan. Established in 1949, RPSC conducts recruitment examinations, interviews and selection processes for various civil services and administrative positions in Rajasthan. The commission ensures transparent and merit-based selection for government jobs across the state.

Significance of RPSC Recruitment 2025

This massive recruitment drive for 12,121 positions is one of the largest in Rajasthan's history. The vacancies span across multiple departments including:

  • Police Department (Sub-Inspector/Platoon Commander)
  • Education Department (TGT and PGT Teachers)
  • Animal Husbandry Department (Veterinary Officers)
  • Agriculture Department (Assistant Agriculture Engineers)

Recruitment Insight:

This recruitment provides excellent opportunities for candidates across various educational backgrounds. With positions available at different levels, both fresh graduates and experienced professionals can find suitable opportunities.

Selection Process

Stages of Selection

  1. Written Examination: Objective type test covering subject knowledge, general awareness, reasoning, and language skills
  2. Physical Efficiency Test (for SI): Running, long jump, high jump and endurance tests
  3. Interview/Document Verification: For shortlisted candidates
  4. Medical Examination: Especially for SI and Veterinary Officer positions

Preparation Strategy

  1. Understand Exam Pattern: Thoroughly analyze the syllabus and marking scheme
  2. Create Study Plan: Allocate time for each subject based on weightage
  3. Study Materials:
    • Rajasthan GK by Dr. G.K. Publications

    • Lucent's General Knowledge

  4. Practice Previous Papers: Solve last 10 years question papers

  5. Upload Documents:

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can candidates from other states apply for RPSC recruitment?

Yes, candidates from other states can apply for most positions, but they will be treated as Unreserved (UR) category candidates and won't be eligible for reservation benefits. However, for some posts like TGT/PGT teachers, knowledge of Rajasthani culture and Hindi language may be required.

2. Is there any interview for RPSC recruitment?

For most positions, selection is based on written examination only. However, for higher-level posts like Veterinary Officer and Assistant Agriculture Engineer, there might be an interview round after the written examination. The specific selection process varies by post as mentioned in the official notification.

3. What is the validity of One Time Registration (OTR)?

The OTR registration on RPSC portal is valid for 5 years from the date of registration. Candidates can apply for multiple recruitments during this period without creating a new registration. However, they need to update their profile periodically, especially before applying for new positions.

4. Is there any physical test for teacher positions?

No, there is no physical test for teaching positions like TGT and PGT. Physical Efficiency Test is only required for Sub Inspector/Platoon Commander positions in Rajasthan Police. Teaching positions selection is based solely on academic qualifications and written examination performance.

5. What is the probation period for RPSC positions?

The probation period varies by position:

  • Sub Inspector: 2 years
  • Teachers: 2 years
  • Veterinary Officer: 2 years
  • Assistant Agriculture Engineer: 2 years
During probation, performance is evaluated for confirmation in service.

6. How to prepare for Rajasthan GK section?

Focus on these key areas:

  1. History: Ancient civilizations, Rajput clans, freedom movement in Rajasthan
  2. Geography: Climate, rivers, national parks, soil types
  3. Culture: Festivals, folk dances, music, art and architecture
  4. Administration: Districts, divisions, current ministers
  5. Economy: Major industries, agriculture, mineral resources
  6. Current Affairs: Recent government schemes, appointments, events
Refer to "Rajasthan Ek Parichay" by Dr. Laxminarayan Sharma for comprehensive coverage.

7. What is the salary structure during probation?

During probation, candidates receive basic pay along with dearness allowance but may not receive all allowances. Typically, the initial salary during probation is about 80% of the regular starting salary. After successful completion of probation, employees receive full salary with all allowances.

8. Are there any coaching allowances provided?

RPSC does not provide any coaching allowances. However, the Rajasthan government offers free coaching for SC/ST/OBC candidates through various schemes. Candidates should check with the Social Justice and Empowerment Department or Tribal Area Development Department for available coaching schemes.

9. How many attempts are allowed for RPSC exams?

There is no fixed number of attempts for most RPSC exams. Candidates can appear until they reach the maximum age limit for the respective post. However, for some specific posts, there might be attempt limits which will be clearly mentioned in the notification.

10. What documents are required during document verification?

Candidates should keep these documents ready:

  1. Original and photocopies of educational certificates
  2. Category certificate (if applicable)
  3. Domicile certificate
  4. Photo identity proof (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID)
  5. RPSC application printout
  6. Character certificate from last institution/employer
  7. Medical fitness certificate (for certain posts)
  8. Two passport-size photographs
