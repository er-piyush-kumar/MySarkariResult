How to Fill MP ESB Excise Constable Online Form 2025

  • Application Window: The online application for the Excise Constable Direct and Backlog Post Recruitment Test - 2024 will be open from 15 February 2025 to 01 March 2025. Make sure to submit your form before the deadline.
  • Create Your Profile: Before applying, you must create a profile on the Madhya Pradesh Professional Board’s portal.
    • If you have a registered mobile number linked to your Aadhaar card, you can complete e-KYC directly.
    • If your mobile number is not linked, you must visit an MP Online Kiosk and complete a biometric profile creation.
  • Register in MP Employment Office: Along with creating your profile, all candidates are required to register in the Madhya Pradesh Employment Office. This is mandatory for the MPESB recruitment process.
  • Check Eligibility & Guidelines: Review the official notification for details on educational qualifications, age limit, and physical standards. Ensure you meet all criteria before submitting your form.
  • Complete the Form: Fill in all personal and academic details accurately. Upload necessary documents, if required, such as scanned photographs or certificates.
  • Fee Payment: Pay the examination fee online (Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking) or via cash at an authorized Kiosk. The portal fee is already included in the total amount.
  • Review & Submit: Double-check your information for any errors. Once you submit, you can still correct mistakes until the correction deadline (06/03/2025), but it’s best to be accurate from the start.
  • Read the Full Notification: For detailed instructions, important dates, and updates, refer to the complete official notification provided by the Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board.
