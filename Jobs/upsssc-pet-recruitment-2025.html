About UPSSSC and PET Exam

What is UPSSSC PET?

The UPSSSC Preliminary Eligibility Test (PET) is a screening examination conducted by the Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) to shortlist candidates for various government job recruitments in Uttar Pradesh.

Why is PET Important?

  • Mandatory qualification for most UPSSSC recruitments
  • Valid for 3 years from declaration of result
  • Serves as first stage for various Group B and Group C posts
  • Eliminates need for preliminary exams for individual recruitments

UPSSSC: Organization Overview

The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) is the premier recruiting agency for Group B and Group C posts in the Uttar Pradesh government. Established in 1988, it conducts recruitment for various departments including:

Department Example Posts
Revenue Department Lekhpal, Revenue Inspector
Health Department ANM, Staff Nurse, Pharmacist
Education Department Assistant Teacher, Junior Assistant
Panchayati Raj Village Development Officer
Technical Services Junior Engineer, Technician

How to Apply for UPSSSC PET 2025

Step-by-Step Application Process

  1. Visit Official Website: Go to UPSSSC recruitment portal (upsssc.gov.in)
  2. New Registration: Click on "Apply Online" for PET 2025 and register
  3. Fill Application Form:
    • Personal Details
    • Educational Qualifications
    • Communication Address
    • Category Details
  4. Upload Documents:
    • Recent Photograph (3.5cm × 4.5cm)
    • Signature (black ink on white paper)
    • ID Proof (Aadhar preferred)
  5. Payment of Fees: Pay through SBI I Collect or E-Challan
  6. Final Submission: Verify all details before final submission
  7. Print Application: Take printout for future reference

Important Notes:

  • Keep scanned copies of all documents ready before starting application
  • Ensure photograph and signature meet specified size and format
  • Note down registration number and password
  • Correction window will be open till 24/06/2025
  • No offline applications will be accepted

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the validity period of UPSSSC PET score?

The UPSSSC PET score is valid for 3 years from the date of result declaration. However, the commission may extend this validity period through official notifications.

2. Is there any negative marking in UPSSSC PET exam?

As per previous year patterns, there is no negative marking in UPSSSC PET exam. However, candidates should verify this in the latest official notification.

3. Can candidates from other states apply for UPSSSC PET?

Yes, candidates from other states can apply for UPSSSC PET. However, for subsequent recruitment processes, some posts may have domicile requirements.

4. What is the minimum qualifying marks for UPSSSC PET?

The minimum qualifying marks vary by category:

  • General/OBC: 40%
  • SC/ST: 35%
  • PH: 30%

These percentages may change as per commission's discretion.

5. How many times can I appear for UPSSSC PET?

There is no restriction on the number of attempts for UPSSSC PET, provided the candidate meets the age criteria for each attempt.

6. What documents are required during application?

Essential documents include:

  • Scanned photograph and signature
  • Class 10 certificate (or higher qualification proof)
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile certificate (for reserved category candidates)
  • PH certificate (if applicable)
7. What is the exam center selection process?

Candidates can select their preferred exam district during application. Final exam center allocation is done by UPSSSC based on availability. Requests for changes after submission are generally not entertained.

8. How can I prepare for General Hindi section?

For General Hindi preparation:

  1. Focus on basic grammar rules
  2. Practice synonyms/antonyms daily
  3. Learn common idioms and phrases
  4. Solve previous year questions
  5. Read Hindi newspapers regularly
9. What are the job opportunities after qualifying PET?

After qualifying PET, candidates become eligible for various posts including:

  • Lekhpal
  • Junior Assistant
  • Staff Nurse
  • Village Development Officer
  • Junior Engineer
  • Lab Technician
  • And many other Group B and C posts
10. How can I check my PET result?

PET results will be available on UPSSSC official website. Candidates need to:

  1. Visit upsssc.gov.in
  2. Click on 'Results' section
  3. Find PET 2025 result link
  4. Enter registration details
  5. View/download scorecard
