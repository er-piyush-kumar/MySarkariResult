Army Agniveer CEE Online Form 2025 | Apply Online for Agniveer Posts

Short Information: Indian Army has released the notification for the Agniveer Common Entrance Exam (CEE) 2025. Eligible candidates can apply online from 12 March 2025 to 10 April 2025. Read the notification for eligibility, selection process, and other details.

Important Dates

Application Begin: 12/03/2025
Last Date for Apply Online: 10/04/2025
Exam Date: June 2025
Admit Card Available: Before Exam

Application Fee

  • General / OBC / EWS: ₹250/-
  • SC / ST: ₹250/-
  • Payment Mode: Online (Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking)

Eligibility Criteria : Age Limit Details 2025

Post Name Age Limit
Agniveer GD / Technical / Assistant / Tradesman 17.5 to 21 Years

Vacancy Details and Eligibility 2025

Havildar Education

Education Qualification :

  • IT / Cyber: Bachelor / Master Degree with 50% Minimum Marks. (BCA / MCA / B.Tech / B.SC / M.SC IT / AI / ML / Data Analytics / Data Science Information Security / Equivalent)
  • Information Operations: Bachelor / Master Degree with 50% Minimum Marks. (Bachelor / Master Degree in Information Technology / Computer Science / Mass Communication / Journalism / Psychology / Sociology / Public Administration / Political Science / Military Study and Defence Management)
  • Linguist: Bachelor / Master Degree with 50% Marks (BA / MA in Foreign Languages Chinese / Myanmar)

Physical Eligibility Group I:
Height: Region Wise.
More details in the official notification.

Havildar Surveyor Automated Cartographer

  • Education Qualification: BA / B.SC with Mathematics and 10+2 with PCM as Main Subjects with Minimum 50% Marks in Each OR Bachelor Degree in Engineering (BE / B.Tech) in Civil / Electronic / Electrical / Instrumentation / Mechanical / Computer Technology / Computer Science with 10+2 Intermediate Pass in PCM Group of Subjects and 50% Marks in Each.
  • Additional Qualification: Diploma / Certificate in Cookery / Hotel Management and Catering Technology.

Physical Eligibility Group I:
Height: Region Wise.
More details in the official notification.

JCO Catering

  • Education Qualification: 10+2 Intermediate Exam in Any Recognized Board in India.
  • Additional Qualification: Diploma / Certificate in Cookery / Hotel Management and Catering Technology.

Physical Eligibility Group I:
Height: Region Wise.
More details in the official notification.

JCO Religious Teacher (Dharm Guru) Pandit, Pandit Gorkha, Granthi, Maulvi, Padre, Bodh Monk

  • RT Pandit and Pandit Gorkha: Hindu Candidate with Shastri / Acharya in Sanskrit (Karam Kand) as Main Subject OR One Year Diploma in Karam Kand.
  • RT Granthi: Sikh Candidate with Bachelor Degree in Any Stream and Gyani in Punjabi.
  • RT Maulvi: Muslim Candidate with Bachelor Degree in Any Stream and Alim in Arabic or Adeeb-e-Mahir/Urdu Mahir in Urdu.
  • RT Padre: Christian Candidate with Bachelor Degree in Any Stream.
  • RT Buddhist: Buddhist Candidate with Bachelor Degree in Any Stream.

Physical Eligibility Group I:
Height: General - 160 CM, Gorkhas & Ladakhi Region - 157 CM.
Chest: 77 CM.
1.6 Km Running: 8 Min.
More details in the official notification.
