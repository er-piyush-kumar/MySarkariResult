About IDBI Bank and JAM Role

What is IDBI Bank?

IDBI Bank (Industrial Development Bank of India) is one of India's leading public sector banks and financial service providers. Established in 1964, it became a scheduled commercial bank in 2004. Currently, LIC holds majority stake in IDBI Bank.

Junior Assistant Manager (JAM) Role

The Junior Assistant Manager (Grade O) is an entry-level position in IDBI Bank with responsibilities including:

  • Customer service and relationship management
  • Handling banking transactions and operations
  • Account opening and KYC documentation
  • Cross-selling bank products and services
  • Assisting in branch administration
  • Cash management and teller operations

Career Insight:

JAM is the first step in IDBI Bank's officer cadre with excellent growth opportunities to Assistant Manager, Manager and higher positions through promotions and internal exams.

How to Prepare for IDBI JAM Exam 2025

Preparation Strategy

  1. Understand the Syllabus: Thoroughly analyze the exam pattern and syllabus
  2. Create Study Plan: Allocate time for each section daily
  3. Study Materials:
    • Quantitative Aptitude by R.S. Aggarwal
    • Verbal & Non-Verbal Reasoning by R.S. Aggarwal
    • English Grammar & Comprehension books
    • Banking Awareness by Arihant or Kiran's
  4. Practice Mock Tests: Take regular full-length tests
  5. Current Affairs: Daily newspaper reading (Economic Times, Financial Express)
  6. Previous Papers: Solve last 5 years question papers

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the probation period for JAM in IDBI Bank?

The probation period for Junior Assistant Manager (JAM) is typically 2 years, during which performance is evaluated for confirmation.

2. Is there any interview for IDBI JAM selection?

No, the selection is based purely on online examination score followed by document verification and medical test.

3. Can final year students apply for IDBI JAM?

Yes, final year students can apply provisionally but must submit final mark sheet by the specified date mentioned in notification.

4. What is the service bond for JAM in IDBI Bank?

Selected candidates need to execute a bond to serve the bank for minimum 2 years or pay ₹2,00,000 as liquidated damages.

5. Is IDBI JAM a permanent job?

Yes, after successful completion of probation period, JAM becomes a permanent employee of IDBI Bank.

6. What is the posting location policy for JAM?

Selected candidates can be posted anywhere in India as per bank's requirement. Initial posting is usually in rural/semi-urban branches.

7. What is the career growth path after JAM?

Typical career path: JAM → Assistant Manager → Manager → Senior Manager → Chief Manager → Assistant General Manager → Deputy General Manager → General Manager.

8. How to prepare for banking awareness section?

Focus on:

  1. Current banking news (last 6 months)
  2. RBI policies and circulars
  3. Banking terms and abbreviations
  4. Financial and economic surveys
  5. Government schemes related to banking

9. What is the work timing for JAM in IDBI Bank?

General working hours are 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM (may vary by branch), with second and fourth Saturdays off. Extended hours during peak periods.

10. How can I download my IDBI JAM admit card?

Admit cards will be available on IDBI Bank's career portal:

  1. Visit www.idbibank.in/careers
  2. Click on "Download Admit Card"
  3. Enter registration number and password
  4. Download and print admit card
